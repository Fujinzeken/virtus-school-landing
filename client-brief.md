TECHNICAL SPECIFICATION
Website Development for an International School

1. Project Objective
   To develop a modern, premium-level website for a newly established international school operating under international education programmes.
   The main objectives of the website are:
   Create a professional first impression about the school

Build trust among parents

Receive traffic from advertising campaigns

Register potential students

Collect applications and leads

The website will serve as the central element of the school's marketing system.

2. Target Audience
   Primary audience:
   Parents aged 25–50

Families interested in international education

High-income segment in Tashkent

Parents who want their children to prepare for foreign universities
